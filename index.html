The Best NBA Playoff Performances of All Time

The NBA playoffs is where the best players truly shine. Legends are made in the postseason. Throughout the history of the NBA, some of the best players to ever step on the court have had all-time great individual performances in the playoffs. In this list, you will find 10 of the best individual performances in NBA playoff history, from most recent to oldest.

Luka Doncic's Historic Game Winner

Doncic's performance in Game 4 of the Western First Round in 2020 broke so many records that it's impossible to list them all here, so you can find them here instead. His OT buzzer beater against the Clippers capped off a 21 point comeback for the Mavs, the largest in franchise postseason history. His monster triple-double made him only the ninth player to ever record consecutive triples doubles in the playoffs, and only the third at the age of 21 or younger.

LeBron James brings the Cavs back from down 3-1 in the Finals

James' chasedown block of Andre Igoudala in the closing minutes of Game 7 of the 2016 NBA Finals is one of the most iconic moments in NBA history. The moment capped off the Cavs comeback from down 3-1 against the 73-9 Warriors, which made them the only team to ever come back from a 3-1 deficit in the Finals. James' triple-double in Game 7 also capped off a series of huge performances, and allowed him to finally bring a title to Cleveland.

LeBron's Best Playoff Game Ever?

In 2012, two years into the "Big 3" era in Miami, the Heat still hadn't won a championship. And now, they were facing elimination to the Boston Celtics, one of James' long time rivals, in Game 6 of the Eastern Conference Finals. If the Heat lost here, they would have nothing to show two years after bringing 3 All-Stars together. With the pressure on, James had a monster performance, scoring 45 points and grabbing 15 rebounds, pushing the series to a Game 7 back in Miami. The Heat would go on to win Game 7 and the championship, James’ first. This game is considered the greatest of James’ career by many.
